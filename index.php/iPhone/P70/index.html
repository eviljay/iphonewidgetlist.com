Conspiracy
1.5 

Conspiracy plots functions in two or three dimensions. You enter the function by clicking in the lower right corner. To get started, use the ? toolbar icon to view some samples. The other toolbar icons can be used to zoom or reset the graph coordinates. Change the center point of the plot by clicking on the graph.
