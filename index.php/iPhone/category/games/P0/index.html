Word Snake

3.3 

Word Snake is a game all about making connected words. Arrange the sets of letters into adjacent words, where the last letter of one word is the first letter of the next. Words are aligned within the snake shape, starting from the top left and working their way down the length of the snake. The start and end of each word are highlighted.

Lunarware Sudoku
3.8 

This Sudoku puzzle has a minimal user interface designed for iPhones, but easily playable on any conceivable WWW device.

Cells are filled with a single touch.
You can unfill a cell, also with a single touch, if you slipped.
You can't mark up cells.
The puzzles are generated with qqwing.
