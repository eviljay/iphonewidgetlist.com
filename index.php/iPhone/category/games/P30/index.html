Reversi
3.6 

You play by placing a black token on the board so that at least one of your opponent's white tokens is bounded on two sides (horizontally, vertically or diagonally) by your black tokens. At that point all of your opponent's white tokens between your black tokens reverses to black. Your opponent will place white tokens to change your tokens white.

The goal of the game is to finish with more black tokens than white tokens on the board. Be careful - your opponent is tricky!
