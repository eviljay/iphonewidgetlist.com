Launchrz
4.3 

Launchrz is an iPhone application discovery and management tool. Users populate their own custom "Launchrz page" with applications from the extensive directory and can launch them with just one tap. Its free and fully functional without registration, although members get special features such as the ability to rate the applications and access to their Launchrz page from other devices.

Word Rotation
2.7 

Word Rotation is all about making words. Arrange sets of letters into words. With the current options, you get 4 rows of 4-letter words to figure out. Each letter position can contain up to 3 different letters. By pressing a letter, it 'rotates' to the next letter and forms a different word. Make 4 valid words to win.
