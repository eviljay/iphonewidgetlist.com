2.3 

iPhone Feeds is a proof of concept for a Feed Reader iPhone Application. Simply hit the "+" sign to add the URL of a feed. Feeds can be removed by hitting "edit". Built with PHP, AJAX and MagpieRSS, it's a lightweight and easy to use rss feed reader optimized for the iPhone.

Avalanche is a great simple game that fits the iPhone. There's no directions and it took a minute to figure out. The idea is to touch a group of 3 or more contiguous block of the same color and they will disappear, causing the ones above to fall and take their place.
