2.2 

It's a clone of a popular "match the colored gems" game you may have played before. Click a colored square, then an adjacent square to swap them. Line up 3 or more gems in a horizontal or vertical line to score! Your first move starts the timer.

I think I still need to fine-tune the scoring mechanism and some other things, but it basically works.
